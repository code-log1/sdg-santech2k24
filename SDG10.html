Goal 10: Reduced Inequalities

Reduce inequality within and among countries.

Together Towards Equality

Reducing inequalities is vital for sustainable development. By ensuring equal opportunities and promoting social, economic, and political inclusion, we can create a fairer world for all.

Empower and Promote Social, Economic, and Political Inclusion

Ensure equal opportunity and reduce inequalities of outcome by eliminating discriminatory laws, policies, and practices.

Achieve and Sustain Income Growth

By 2030, achieve sustained and inclusive economic growth for the bottom 40% of the population at a rate higher than the national average.

Ensure Equal Access to Resources

Ensure equal access to economic resources, as well as access to basic services, technology, and financial services.
