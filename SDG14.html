Goal 14: Life Below Water

Conserve and sustainably use the oceans, seas, and marine resources for sustainable development.

Protecting Our Oceans

The oceans are vital for our planet and humanity. We must take action to preserve marine life, protect marine ecosystems, and sustainably manage our oceans.

Reducing Marine Pollution

By 2025, prevent and significantly reduce marine pollution of all kinds, in particular from land-based activities.

Protecting Coastal and Marine Areas

Ensure the conservation of coastal and marine areas through effective and integrated management.

Promoting Sustainable Fishing

By 2020, prohibit certain forms of fisheries subsidies that contribute to overcapacity and overfishing.
