Goal 16: Peace, Justice, and Strong Institutions

Promote peaceful and inclusive societies for sustainable development, provide access to justice for all, and build effective, accountable, and inclusive institutions at all levels.

Building a Just and Peaceful Society

Peace, justice, and strong institutions are essential for sustainable development. Together, we can foster inclusive societies, promote justice for all, and strengthen institutions.

Reducing Violence and Related Death Rates

Significantly reduce all forms of violence and related death rates everywhere.

Promoting the Rule of Law

Promote the rule of law at the national and international levels and ensure equal access to justice for all.

Strengthening Institutions

Substantially reduce corruption and bribery in all their forms and develop effective, accountable, and transparent institutions.
