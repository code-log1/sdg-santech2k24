Goal 5: Gender Equality

Achieve gender equality and empower all women and girls.

End discrimination against women and girls everywhere

Eradicate all forms of women discrimination in all places.

End all violence and exploitation of girls

Eliminate all forms of violence against all women and girls in the public and private spheres, including trafficking and other types of exploitation.

Ensure full participation in leadership and decision making
