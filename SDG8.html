Goal 8: Decent Work and Economic Growth

Promote sustained, inclusive, and sustainable economic growth, full and productive employment, and decent work for all.

Fostering Economic Growth and Decent Work

Decent work and economic growth are essential for sustainable development. By fostering inclusive economic growth, we can create job opportunities that empower individuals and communities. It is crucial to ensure that everyone has access to meaningful employment that offers fair wages, safe working conditions, and respect for rights.

Promote Sustainable Economic Growth

By 2030, aim for at least 7% GDP growth per year in the least developed countries.

Decent Work for All

Ensure equal access to decent work opportunities, particularly for young people and women, promoting policies that encourage entrepreneurship and job creation.

Support Small and Medium Enterprises

Encourage the growth of micro, small, and medium-sized enterprises (MSMEs) and their integration into the global economy to enhance employment opportunities.
